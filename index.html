Hi, I'm Opey.

I tend to overdo things. A thrilling and very relevant example is this very account, posting my notes, as it was. This account was quite literally designed to be the direct antithesis to my normal “spend a month making a single animation because it needs to be crisp” attitude. And yet, even despite this, not even a month in and I was already pressuring myself to write these long, massive brain essays. Alas, I was overdoing it. After a while, the very activity I had designed to be a wind-down became a tedious task I had to do. It sounded like work and, naturally, it floated down into the abyss of my tasks. Sometimes, overdoing it literally isn’t worth it.
